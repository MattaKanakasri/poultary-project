About This Project

This AI-based poultry disease detection system uses a deep learning model that classifies images of chicken droppings into four categories: Coccidiosis, Newcastle Disease, Salmonella, and Healthy. The project helps farmers receive instant diagnosis using a simple image upload. It aims to reduce economic losses due to delayed treatment and improve poultry health management across rural and urban areas.
